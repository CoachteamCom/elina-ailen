About the Designer

Emerging from the creative heart of Córdoba, Argentina, Elina Ailen transforms fabric into narrative. Her collections breathe life into Victorian romanticism, reimagined for the modern muse.

Each piece is a meditation on femininity — delicate lace panels, structured silhouettes, and the interplay between revelation and concealment. Her work speaks to those who dress not merely to be seen, but to be felt.

Drawing inspiration from antique textiles, classical portraiture, and the ethereal quality of dreams, she creates garments that exist between eras — timeless yet unmistakably contemporary.
